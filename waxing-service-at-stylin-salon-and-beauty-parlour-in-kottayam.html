Why Choose Waxing?

Waxing is a highly effective hair removal method that removes hair from the root, offering longer-lasting smoothness compared to shaving. It also reduces hair regrowth over time, leading to finer and softer hair.

Pre-Waxing Care

  • Exfoliate your skin a day before your appointment to remove dead skin cells.
  • Ensure your hair is at least 1/4 inch long for effective waxing.
  • Avoid applying lotions, oils, or creams before waxing.
  • Wear loose clothing to prevent irritation after your session.

Post-Waxing Care

  • Avoid hot showers, steam, and sun exposure for at least 24 hours.
  • Moisturize your skin with aloe vera or a soothing lotion.
  • Exfoliate gently after 48 hours to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Wear breathable clothing to keep your skin irritation-free.
