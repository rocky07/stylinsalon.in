Why the Right Haircut Matters

Round faces are known for their soft, curved lines and equal width and height. The goal is to add angles and length to balance the natural roundness. With the right cut, you can create the illusion of a more elongated or defined face shape.

Top Haircuts for Round Faces at Stylin Salon

  • Long Layers: Add volume and definition without bulk around the cheeks. Long layers create movement and visually lengthen the face.
  • Angled Bob: This sleek and modern cut is shorter in the back and longer in the front, which draws attention away from roundness and adds shape.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Soft side-swept bangs break the roundness and frame your face beautifully. They work great with medium or long hair.
  • Shag Cut: A modern shag adds texture and volume in the right places, especially with feathered layers and wispy ends.
  • Straight with Volume on Top: Keeping the sides smooth and adding volume at the crown gives a more oval appearance—perfect for round faces!
