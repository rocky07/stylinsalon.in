At Stylin Ladies Salon, we strive to provide the best beauty and wellness services to our customers. Due to the nature of our services, we have the following Return & Refund Policy in place:

1. Service Refund Policy

  • All services provided at Stylin Ladies Salon are non-refundable once completed.
  • If you are dissatisfied with a service, please inform us before leaving the salon, and we will do our best to resolve the issue.
  • Any adjustments or corrections must be requested within 24 hours of the service, and we will offer a complimentary touch-up if necessary.

2. Advance Payments & Cancellations

  • Appointments booked with an advance payment can be rescheduled if notified at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows will result in a non-refundable advance payment.

3. Product Return Policy

  • Unopened and unused retail products purchased from our salon may be returned within 7 days of purchase for an exchange or store credit.
  • Opened or used products cannot be returned due to hygiene reasons.
  • Defective or damaged products may be replaced if reported within 48 hours of purchase.

4. Gift Cards & Packages

  • Gift cards and prepaid packages are non-refundable but can be transferred to another individual.
  • Expired gift cards or packages cannot be redeemed.

5. Special Cases

  • Refunds may be considered only in cases of allergic reactions or service-related issues with valid proof (such as medical reports or immediate photo evidence).
